About me

I'm Rajesh Phulwaria... a self-driven developer who jumped into coding recently but refuses to think small. I build projects that escalate quickly: from simple Java and JS utilities to graph plotters, 3D simulations in Blender, computer-vision experiments, and space-trajectory ideas inspired by ISRO/NASA. I don't do “safe” or boring. If something sparks my curiosity, I push it until it becomes a working prototype... whether that's orbit simulators, UI/UX concepts generated with AI, or practical tools like billing systems, Instagram viewers, and gesture-recognition programs. I work like a builder who'd rather break things than stay stagnant. My brain is always trying to turn real-life patterns into algorithms, and I'm comfortable letting obsession drive progress. Outside of coding. If you're reading this, expect projects that aren't polished for show... they're built to learn, to explore, and to stand out.
